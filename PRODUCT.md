# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders and recruiters evaluating Aakarsh Singh for engineering roles where product judgment, ownership, and execution matter. They need to understand his positioning, credibility, and production impact within seconds.

## Product Purpose

Present Aakarsh as a full-stack and AI engineer who turns ambitious product ideas into dependable systems used by real people. Success means qualified visitors quickly see evidence of ownership and scale, explore the work, and start a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Inventive, formidable, and assured. The voice is direct and specific: ambitious without posturing, technical without hiding behind jargon, and energetic without becoming noisy.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS landing pages, AI-generated gradient-and-glass aesthetics, decorative developer terminals, stat-card dashboards, anonymous stock imagery, and overproduced effects that obscure the work.

## Design Principles

- Lead with a sharp point of view, not a list of technologies.
- Make production impact visible before asking visitors to trust a claim.
- Treat Aakarsh as the protagonist; the interface should feel personal and authored.
- Use motion to create momentum and hierarchy, never as ambient decoration.
- Give founders and recruiters a clear path from intrigue to evidence to contact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and keyboard usability. Respect reduced-motion preferences, preserve readable type across viewport sizes, and never rely on color or animation alone to communicate meaning.
